Structured e-commerce operations for modern brands

Blue Meridian Ltd is a UK-based company focused on sourcing, managing and distributing low-risk consumer products through online marketplaces such as Amazon and other e-commerce channels.

What we do

We operate behind the scenes of digital retail, building product lines, managing marketplace accounts and coordinating logistics so our partners can focus on growth.

Product sourcing

Identification and sourcing of high-demand, low-risk consumer products in categories such as beauty, personal care, home & kitchen and lifestyle accessories.

Marketplace management

Setup and optimisation of listings, pricing and catalogue structure on Amazon and other online marketplaces, always aligned with compliance requirements.

Logistics coordination

Coordination of storage and fulfilment using third-party warehousing and FBA-style services for efficient, trackable deliveries within the United Kingdom.

Categories we work with

Our focus is on safe, compliant and fast-moving product categories, including:

  • Beauty & personal care
  • Skincare and wellness accessories
  • Home & kitchen essentials
  • Organisers and storage products
  • Lifestyle and everyday accessories
  • Non-electronic or low-risk light accessories
