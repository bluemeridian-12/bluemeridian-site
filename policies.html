Business policies

Governance, compliance and business information for Blue Meridian Ltd.

Overview

Blue Meridian Ltd operates as a UK registered e-commerce company focused on developing and managing its own consumer products within the Amazon FBA marketplace. This page consolidates our key policies regarding privacy, terms of service, returns, warranty and compliance.

These policies apply to institutional communication, supplier relationships and visitors to our corporate website.

Privacy Policy

Blue Meridian Ltd handles personal and business information in accordance with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018. We collect only what is necessary to respond to institutional enquiries, supplier communication or compliance matters.

Information we may collect:

  • Name and contact details provided voluntarily
  • Business information relevant to partnership discussions
  • Technical data such as browser type or access time (standard server logs)

We do not sell, rent or distribute personal information to third parties. Data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil its purpose or meet legal requirements.

For privacy requests, contact: Vitor@bluemeridian.uk

Terms of Service

This website provides institutional information about Blue Meridian Ltd. All content is for general business reference and may be updated without prior notice.

By using this website, you acknowledge the following:

  • The site does not sell products directly to the public
  • No commercial agreements are created through browsing or contacting us
  • All trademarks, logos and materials remain property of Blue Meridian Ltd
  • Unauthorised reproduction or misuse of content is prohibited

Any formal agreement with suppliers or partners must be documented separately and signed by authorised representatives of Blue Meridian Ltd.

Returns & Warranty

Blue Meridian Ltd sells its products exclusively through Amazon marketplaces. As a result, all returns, exchanges and warranty claims are processed under:

  • Amazon’s Returns Policy
  • Amazon’s A-to-Z Guarantee (where applicable)
  • The UK Consumer Rights Act 2015

Customers should initiate any request directly through their Amazon order page. We do not process returns or refunds via email or through this website.

For institutional issues related to product quality or documentation, contact: Vitor@bluemeridian.uk

Compliance Statement

Blue Meridian Ltd is committed to operating in accordance with UK law, marketplace regulations and product-specific safety standards. All products are sourced from verified suppliers and accompanied by the required documentation for their category.

  • Verification of supplier legitimacy and manufacturing standards
  • Compliance checks for product labeling, safety information and packaging
  • Documentation consistent with UK and EU requirements where applicable
  • Ongoing monitoring of regulatory updates affecting consumer products

Supply Chain Policy

Our supply chain principles are based on transparency, documentation and long-term reliability. We expect suppliers to meet reasonable standards related to quality control, responsible sourcing and legal compliance.

Key principles:

  • Clear communication of specifications and requirements prior to production
  • Quality inspections and sample approval before mass manufacturing
  • Accurate documentation for import, safety and category compliance
  • Respect for ethical labour practices and local regulations
  • Cooperation in audit or documentation requests when necessary

Our intention is to establish responsible, documented and mutually beneficial supplier relationships.
