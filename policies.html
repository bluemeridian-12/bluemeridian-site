Business policies

Blue Meridian operates with a focus on regulatory compliance, transparency and responsible supply chains.

Policy framework

As a UK registered business, Blue Meridian aligns its operations with applicable legislation and marketplace rules. The following policy areas are part of our governance framework:

  • Terms of service for our corporate website and any direct interactions with counterparties.
  • Privacy policy covering the handling of contact details and business information shared with us.
  • Compliance statement summarising our commitment to applicable UK and EU regulations for consumer products.
  • Supply chain policy addressing expectations towards suppliers on quality, documentation and ethical standards.
  • Returns and warranty which apply to products sold through Amazon, in line with Amazon policies and local consumer law.

Detailed documents and additional information can be provided on request for legitimate institutional or regulatory purposes.
