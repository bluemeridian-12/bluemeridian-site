Returns & refunds

Blue Meridian Ltd primarily sells through online marketplaces such as Amazon. When a purchase is made through a marketplace, returns and refunds are processed according to that marketplace’s policies and the applicable consumer law in the country of purchase.

Customers should initiate any return or refund request directly through the marketplace where the order was placed, using the tools provided on that platform.

Shipping & delivery

Orders are typically fulfilled using marketplace fulfilment solutions or third-party logistics providers. Delivery times, tracking information and shipping options depend on the marketplace, the customer’s location and the product involved.

Specific delivery information is shown on each listing at the time of purchase.

Privacy policy

We only collect and process the personal data necessary to operate our business, manage orders and comply with legal obligations. When sales occur through marketplaces, most customer data is processed inside those platforms in line with their own privacy policies.

We do not sell or share customer data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be stored with service providers that support our operations (for example, logistics, IT or cloud infrastructure providers), always under appropriate safeguards.

If you would like to request access to, correction of or deletion of personal data held by Blue Meridian Ltd, please contact us using the details on the Contact page.
