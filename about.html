About Blue Meridian Ltd

Blue Meridian Ltd is a private limited company based in London, United Kingdom, focused on building structured, compliant e-commerce operations for consumer products.

Our work happens mostly behind the scenes: we design product ranges, connect with suppliers, prepare marketplace structures and coordinate logistics so that the final buying experience is simple and reliable for end customers.

The company has a long-term strategy of growing a portfolio of low-risk consumer products across categories like beauty, personal care, home & lifestyle, always working within marketplace and regulatory requirements.

How we operate

Our operating model is centred on:

  • Careful selection of compliant, low-risk products suitable for online sales.
  • Use of established third-party logistics and warehousing providers.
  • Adherence to marketplace rules and local regulations in every market.
  • Continuous review of product performance, demand and compliance.
