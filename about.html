About Blue Meridian Ltd

Blue Meridian Ltd is a private limited company registered in England and Wales and focused on building structured e-commerce operations for consumer products.

Our role is to design and run the operational backbone of online retail: product sourcing, marketplace setup, catalogue optimisation and logistics coordination using third-party fulfilment solutions such as FBA-style warehouses.

The company currently operates from London, United Kingdom, with a long-term strategy of expanding its portfolio of brands and product lines while maintaining a low-risk, compliance-first approach.

How we operate

  • We work only with safe, compliant and legal product categories.
  • We rely on established third-party logistics and warehousing providers.
  • We follow marketplace policies and regional regulations for each market where we sell.
  • We continuously review performance, demand and compliance before expanding any range.
