About Blue Meridian

A focused e-commerce company building and operating consumer products for the Amazon FBA UK marketplace.

Who we are

Blue Meridian Ltd is a UK registered e-commerce company dedicated to developing, acquiring and operating consumer products within the Amazon marketplace. We act as operators, not as a service agency. Our work is centred on our own portfolio of products and brands.

The company was created with a simple premise: run a lean, disciplined and transparent operation, with clear processes and a long term view on every product line we launch.

Our model

We focus on Amazon FBA UK as our primary sales channel. Instead of trying to be present everywhere at once, we concentrate resources on understanding the dynamics of this specific marketplace and operating within it with precision.

  • Own brands and product lines only
  • Data guided product selection
  • Verified suppliers and structured documentation
  • Integrated FBA logistics and stock management

Not an agency

We do not offer account management services and we do not operate third party brands. All of our internal processes are designed around the responsibility of owning the products, the stock and the customer relationship through Amazon.

Vision and approach

Vision

Build a resilient and compliant portfolio of consumer products inside Amazon UK, with operations that are transparent, efficient and sustainable over time.

We prioritise durability over hype: categories with consistent demand, products that solve practical needs and operations that can be replicated with discipline.

How we work

  • Start with market and unit economics before launching any product
  • Document each stage of the supply chain and compliance process
  • Monitor operational KPIs on a recurring basis
  • Scale gradually, based on proven results, not assumptions

Governance and compliance

Operating as a UK company means aligning to local regulation and fiscal standards, as well as to Amazon marketplace policies. Governance is treated as a core element of our model, not as an afterthought.

  • Blue Meridian Ltd · Company number 16884563
  • Registered in England and Wales
  • Regular accounting and tax reporting
  • Category specific compliance for consumer products

Supplier relationships

We work to maintain clear, documented and long term oriented relationships with our suppliers, including expectations on quality, timelines, documentation and ethical standards.

A small, focused operation

Blue Meridian is intentionally built as a compact structure. This allows for direct decision making, fast iteration and close control over each operational stage.

As the company grows, we plan to expand our portfolio and team gradually, maintaining the same principles of discipline, transparency and long term orientation.
